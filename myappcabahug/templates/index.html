What is HeViCa??

Established in 2021, HeViCa (Reservation System) contains modern designed conference rooms and have the latest models in technology to provide best experience for the meeting attendees.

HeViCa (Reservation System) provides best environment for every important matters.

Our Goals

The goal of the HeViCa (Reservation System) is to provide a conference room and utilize the use of facilities while maintaining a high-quality environment for important meetings for hospital employees in which they can communicate effectively and discuss private matters.

Our Services

Projectors, speakers, microphones, monitors, stadiums, and even food for customers are among the services provided.

Our Establishments

We are growing our facilities and attempting to accommodate as many hospitals or clinics as possible; however, we currently only have one location where all conference room reservations can be made.

Features

Our Commitment to Your Safety

As we navigate through the new normal, HeViCa remains committed to maintain the wellness services for safe and worry-free service to our rooms with temperature checks and air humidifier disinfectant equipments.

Rapid Testing for Faster Result

Our facility provides rapid testing kits to help individuals determine their Covid test result for the pandemic protocol. After test results are in, our tool can help interpret test results faster and guide for the next steps.
